Welcome to Renu Oil

RENU OIL OF AMERICA, INC. is a Las Vegas based corporation. We have specialized in working with many hotels and casinos in Las Vegas for over 12 years, offering extensive experience in oil and grease filtration, as well as asset reclamation and dock management.

  • Aluminum
  • Cardboard/Paper
  • Cooking Oil/Grease
  • Food Waste
  • Glass
  • Plastic
